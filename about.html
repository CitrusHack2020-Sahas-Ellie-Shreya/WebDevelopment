Prison-19

It is necessary that Governor Newsom passes a bill to protect the incarcerated people in California. The U.S. has the highest incarceration rate and it is worrying how dehumanized people in the system become. Many jails and prisons do not have the adequate funds and means to protect those in their custody. There have been new evidence that many cannot even obtain basic necessities such as soap and proper showers. During one of the most catastrophic times we must not turn a bild eye to those who are incarcerated.

We want this website to be a way of bringing attention to the issues without prison system. The lack of empathy from people in power to those who are most affected is appalling. Speak up for those who cannot and continue practice social distancing.

Thank you.
